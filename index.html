About me

Hi! I’m Vaishnavi, a self‑taught resin artist from Mangaluru. I love mixing pigments, inks, and pressed flowers to create functional art that feels personal—whether it’s an ocean‑wave coaster set or a keepsake with your initials.

I work with professional‑grade epoxy resin (low VOC, food‑safe once cured) and hand‑finish every piece. Minor bubbles or swirls are part of the handmade charm.
